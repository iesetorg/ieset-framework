# Steelman — price_signal_freedom_inflation_volatility_panel

The strongest counterargument is mechanical overlap: Heritage monetary freedom
already penalizes inflation as well as price controls, so its association with
inflation volatility may partly reflect the construction of the index.

Administrative price measures can sometimes smooth temporary shocks, especially
when paired with credible fiscal backing or strategic reserves. A panel
association cannot distinguish harmful repression from short-run stabilization.

This candidate should be read as a broad institutional-price-signal test. A
stronger version should use direct legal price-control coding and separate
price-control effects from monetary-policy credibility.
