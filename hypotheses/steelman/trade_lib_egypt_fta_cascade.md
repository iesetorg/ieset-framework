# Steelman — Egypt FTA cascade

## The strongest opposing argument

1. **QIZ rents, not trade liberalisation.** The QIZ apparel surge
   reflected rent-seeking under Israeli content rules and
   subsidised inputs rather than genuine trade-creation; the
   structure unwound after 2010 as US apparel quotas and
   preferences shifted.

2. **Currency-peg shocks dominate.** The 2003, 2016, and 2022
   currency-peg breaks produced large trade-share variation that
   swamps FTA effects.

3. **Comparator weakness.** Tunisia experienced revolutionary
   disruption; Morocco is the natural comparator but is itself
   covered by a separate FTA-cascade spec.

## What the framework still captures

The descriptive trajectory test records the pattern (rise, peak,
post-revolution dynamics) without claiming pure FTA causation.
The macro-instability critique is acknowledged in the disclosure.
