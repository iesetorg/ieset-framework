# Steelman - uk_furlough_2020_unemployment_output_shield

The steelman against the claim is that furlough was only one part of a bundled policy package; unemployment may have been contained by labour-force exits, reopening timing, and measurement conventions rather than the scheme itself.

The event-window verdict is descriptive and should not be read as a full structural model. Concurrent policy changes, global shocks, and measurement revisions can all weaken causal attribution.
