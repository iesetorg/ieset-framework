# Steelman — Indonesia Omnibus 2020

The strongest counter-reading: the Omnibus was passed in a hurry, partially struck down by the Constitutional Court in 2021 as procedurally defective, and re-issued by Perppu in 2022, so the actual treatment timing is ambiguous and the post-period bites unevenly across firms by sector. KSPI/KSPSI union data argue that fixed-term contract usage rose sharply post-Omnibus, with workers cycling through 5-year fixed-term contracts that the law allows in place of permanent employment — formalisation by re-labelling rather than expansion. Aswicahyono and Manning 2024 evaluations show informality fell only marginally, and where formalisation occurred it was often associated with reduced severance entitlements rather than improved labour-market outcomes for affected workers. The COVID overlap means the synthetic-control donor pool absorbs an enormous shock and the residual treatment effect is small relative to the noise band. A v2 should use within-Indonesia provincial variation in implementation intensity for cleaner identification.
