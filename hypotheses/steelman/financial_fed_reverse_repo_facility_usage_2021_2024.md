# Steelman — Fed ON RRP usage 2021-2024

## The strongest opposing argument

The ON RRP-usage spec is so close to a definitional fact (the data series exists, the peak and trough are observable on FRED) that calling it a "hypothesis" stretches the term. The interesting questions — whether ON RRP usage signals dysfunction in money-market plumbing, whether MMF concentration in ON RRP suppresses bank deposit competition, whether the post-peak decline reflects a deliberate Treasury issuance choice — require a richer causal model than the descriptive thresholds capture.

## What the framework still captures

Pre-committing to specific peak and decline magnitudes anchors the descriptive narrative around quantitative thresholds rather than vibes-level commentary. If the peak had been USD 1bn or the decline had been USD 100bn, the hypothesis would refute, even if the qualitative "ON RRP is informative" story would have remained.
