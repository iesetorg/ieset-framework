# Steelman: ALMP and EPL Low-Education Unemployment

Active labour-market spending often rises when unemployment is already high, so
simple correlations can punish ALMP for responding to labour-market distress.
Strict EPL may also protect incumbent low-skill workers in some settings.

The interaction design tests a narrower flexicurity claim: ALMP should work best
when it is paired with a labour market that can absorb matched workers.
