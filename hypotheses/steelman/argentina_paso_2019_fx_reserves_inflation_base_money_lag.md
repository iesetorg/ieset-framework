# Steelman - argentina_paso_2019_fx_reserves_inflation_base_money_lag

The strongest countercase is that Argentina in 2019-2020 was a bundle of debt, political, pandemic, and capital-control shocks; base money may be endogenous to crisis liquidity demand rather than the independent inflation cause.

The event-window verdict is descriptive and should not be read as a full structural model. Concurrent policy changes, global shocks, and measurement revisions can all weaken causal attribution.
