# Steelman: BIS Credit Gap Governance Crisis Amplifier

Credit booms need not be destructive when they finance productive catch-up,
housing supply, or durable business investment. Strong regulatory quality may
also be endogenous to rich, stable economies rather than a true moderator.

The test is still useful because the interaction distinguishes ordinary
financial deepening from governance-conditioned fragility. A weak result would
warn against over-reading BIS credit gaps as universal crisis signals.
