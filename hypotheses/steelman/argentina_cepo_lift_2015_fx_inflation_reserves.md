# Steelman - argentina_cepo_lift_2015_fx_inflation_reserves

The steelman is that the official exchange-rate jump mostly marked an accounting realignment from a controlled rate to a more realistic rate, not a clean welfare-improving liberalization shock.

The event-window verdict is descriptive and should not be read as a full structural model. Concurrent policy changes, global shocks, and measurement revisions can all weaken causal attribution.
