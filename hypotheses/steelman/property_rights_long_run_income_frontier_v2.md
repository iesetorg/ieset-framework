# Steelman — property rights frontier v2

The strongest counter is that cross-sectional Rule of Law is too collinear with
initial income to identify a clean growth channel. Richer countries both have
stronger institutions and less mechanical catch-up room, which can suppress the
estimated coefficient even if institutional improvements matter within
countries. A weak v2 result is therefore better read as a warning about
between-country identification than as a rejection of property-rights theory.
