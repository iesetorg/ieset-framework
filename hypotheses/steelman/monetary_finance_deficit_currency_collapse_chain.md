# Steelman — Monetary finance is not always catastrophic

**The strongest arguments for monetary finance and against the collapse chain:**

1. **DM QE since 2008 is monetary finance in all but name.** The Fed, ECB, BoE, and BoJ collectively acquired trillions of sovereign debt post-2008 and especially post-2020; by balance-sheet-composition measures this is monetary finance. Inflation remained anchored throughout 2008-2021 in all four. The claim that "monetary finance produces inflation + dollarisation + collapse" is not robust across regimes; institutional quality and expectation anchoring moderate the chain. The hypothesis scopes to EMs with weak CBI, which is defensible but should be stated clearly.

2. **Turkey 2021-2023 was a policy experiment in orthodox-heresy but with partial policy flexibility.** Turkish inflation did spike to 85% and the lira collapsed, but by mid-2023 orthodox policy returned (Şimşek/Erkan) and inflation decelerated without the full Venezuelan chain. Turkey's middle class did not collapse into Venezuela-style emigration; remittance inflows, tourism receipts, and export dynamism kept the economy functional. The chain is attenuated by export-sector diversification.

3. **Sri Lanka's 2020-2022 collapse was dominated by fiscal mismanagement and agricultural policy errors, not monetary finance per se.** The Rajapaksa government's organic-agriculture mandate, VAT cuts, and tourism collapse preceded the monetary-finance response. Attributing the collapse chain to monetary finance rather than to the compound policy failures is an identification reach.

4. **Egypt 2022-2024 macroeconomic adjustment has been painful but managed.** The EGP devaluation, IMF programme, and structural adjustment under Sisi do not match the full "currency collapse" chain; the country has retained capital-market access, foreign-investment inflows (Ras El-Hikma deal 2024 $35B), and has stabilised. The third-order emigration flow from Egypt is limited.

5. **Argentina's dollarisation is a historical phenomenon predating Fernández.** Argentine dollarisation dates to the 1970s; the cepo was first imposed in 2011 under Cristina Kirchner and has been repeatedly re-imposed and removed. Attributing 2019-2023 dollarisation dynamics to monetary finance in that window specifically is contaminated by the decades-long history.

6. **Ghana 2022 faced a commodity-price-driven BoP crisis.** Ghana's FX stress was driven by commodity import-cost shocks (oil, wheat) during 2022, not by domestic monetary finance of a specific magnitude. The IMF programme successfully stabilised without the full collapse chain materialising.

7. **Modern-Monetary-Theory arguments on inflation tolerance.** Under MMT frameworks, inflation is the binding constraint only when productive capacity is reached; in EMs with chronic output gaps, modest monetary finance can fund productive public investment without triggering the collapse chain. This argument has weak empirical support but is not empty; the hypothesis should report the aggregate output-gap statistic per case to allow this critique to bite.

8. **Venezuelan collapse was driven by oil-price collapse + sanctions + PDVSA governance.** The Venezuelan case in the hypothesis sample is so confounded by non-monetary-finance factors that including it as clean evidence is problematic; the case should be robustness only, not primary.

**How this should shape the v1 result card when run:**

- Scope the claim explicitly to EMs with weak central-bank independence; exclude DM QE cases from the primary identification.
- Separate the Venezuelan case into robustness; the oil + sanctions confounds are overwhelming for primary inference.
- Report results for Turkey both pre- and post- the 2023 orthodox return; the turnaround is informative.
- Control for commodity-import-shock (Ghana) and agricultural-policy-disaster (Sri Lanka) confounds explicitly.
- Flag the dollarisation-predated-treatment issue for Argentina; report the marginal effect in the treated window separately from the stock-dollarisation baseline.
- Report output-gap statistics per case to engage the MMT-frame critique honestly.
