# Steelman — state capacity precedes liberal market policy

The strongest counter is that government effectiveness and trade openness are
both outcomes of deeper development processes rather than clean treatments.
High-capacity countries may already be near the frontier, limiting their later
growth premium, while lower-capacity countries can grow quickly from catch-up
even with weaker institutions. A cross-sectional high/low split should
therefore be treated as a screening test that motivates better panel or
event-study work.
