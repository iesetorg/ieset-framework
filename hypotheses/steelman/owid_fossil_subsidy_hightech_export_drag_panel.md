# Steelman: Fossil Subsidies and High-Tech Export Drag

Fossil-fuel subsidies may support industrialization in energy-poor economies or
buffer households during shocks. Resource exporters also have structural reasons
for both high subsidies and lower high-tech exports.

The panel should therefore be read as a lock-in screen, not a definitive causal
estimate. Oil-exporter and income-level robustness checks are essential before
any scoreboard use.
