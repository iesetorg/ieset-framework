# Steelman: Australia high-skill migration

## Strongest case for the claim

Australia's foreign-born employment rate is at parity with native-born — an outcome rarely
observed in European destinations. The skill-stream-dominant system is the candidate
explanation. Cross-country panel evidence on skill-stream-share should reproduce the
within-Australia effect.

## Strongest case against

Australia's labour market is exceptionally tight; foreign-born employment-rate parity may
reflect macroeconomic strength rather than migration policy. Skill-stream-share data is
sparse cross-country.

## Key uncertainties

- Skill-stream-share series construction.

## What would change my mind

Macroeconomic-control absorption of the skill-stream coefficient.
