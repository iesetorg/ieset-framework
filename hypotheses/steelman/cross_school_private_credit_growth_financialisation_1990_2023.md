# Steelman - cross_school_private_credit_growth_financialisation_1990_2023

Claim tested: Private-credit depth does not reliably translate into faster GDP per-capita growth.

The strongest skeptical reading is that broad country-panel associations can be confounded by income level, demography, geography, commodity cycles, or policy endogeneity. This wave is useful for screening whether the claim generalises, not for final causal identification.
