# Steelman - uk_energy_cpi_real_earnings_squeeze_2022

The best counterargument is that energy was not the only inflation driver: reopening demand, global goods shortages, food prices, and sterling weakness all moved with the same timing.

The event-window verdict is descriptive and should not be read as a full structural model. Concurrent policy changes, global shocks, and measurement revisions can all weaken causal attribution.
