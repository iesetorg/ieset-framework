# Steelman: Bangladesh growth, health, and services shift

The strongest caution is that Bangladesh's outcome pattern is not a clean
test of one mechanism. Garments, remittances, NGO-led health and education
programmes, demographic transition, and global market access all overlap.
Services-employment shares may partly reflect informal work classification
rather than high-productivity structural transformation. A supported result
would show that the descriptive development pattern is real, not that the
garments-led model alone caused it.
