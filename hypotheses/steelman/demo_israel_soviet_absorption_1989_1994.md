# Steelman: Israel Soviet absorption 1989-1994

## Strongest case for the claim

The Soviet Jewish inflow was approximately 20% of Israel's pre-immigration population, with
unusually high educational attainment. Israeli tech-cluster growth post-1995 is well-
documented; many founders are Soviet-cohort. TFP gain attribution is plausible.

## Strongest case against

Israeli tech growth was substantially US-Israel-investment-driven (Yozma 1993, BIRD
foundation, US R&D ties). The Soviet cohort may have been a complement rather than primary
driver.

## Key uncertainties

- TFP measurement quality for ISR pre-2000.

## What would change my mind

A null TFP-gain finding would refute the canonical productivity-channel claim.
