# Provenance — monetary_regime_events

**Indicator.** Count of major monetary-regime changes (redenominations,
FX-regime switches, or hyperinflation episodes) in Cuba 1959–2023.

**Series value.** 4 events: 1961, 1994, 2004, 2021. Within the
pre-registered 1993–2023 metric window, 3 events qualify (1994, 2004, 2021).

## Events

### 1. 1961 peso reform ("Operacion P")
- **Type**: redenomination + capital control
- **Authority**: Banco Nacional de Cuba, Castro decree of 4 August 1961.
- **Action**: Overnight withdrawal of pre-revolutionary banknotes; reissue
  capped at 200 pesos per person. Effectively expropriated holdings above
  the cap.
- **Citation**: Mesa-Lago 2014 ch. 1; Pérez-Lopez 1995 *Cuba's Second
  Economy*; original Castro speech 4 August 1961.

### 2. 1994 USD legalisation
- **Type**: FX-regime switch (mono-currency to dual-currency tolerated)
- **Authority**: Decreto-Ley 140 (13 August 1993, in force 1994)
- **Citation**: Gaceta Oficial Extraordinaria No. 13/1993; Mesa-Lago 2014;
  Hidalgo & Vidal 2018.

### 3. 2004 CUC introduction
- **Type**: FX-regime switch (formal dual-currency)
- **Authority**: BCC Resolucion 80/2004 (22 October 2004), 10% USD-to-CUC
  conversion levy.
- **Citation**: BCC Resolucion 80/2004; Vidal Alejandro 2017 "Cuba's
  Monetary Reform"; Mesa-Lago 2014.

### 4. 2021 Tarea Ordenamiento
- **Type**: redenomination + inflation episode
- **Authority**: Decreto-Ley 28/2020 (Comite Ejecutivo Consejo de Ministros);
  effective 1 January 2021.
- **Action**: Retired CUC at 24:1 to CUP. Devalued official CUP from 1:1
  to 24:1 vs USD. Triggered measured inflation of 77% (ONEI) and parallel-
  market inflation of 500%+ (Havana Consulting Group).
- **Citation**: ONEI Cuba *Anuario Estadistico de Cuba 2021*; Vidal 2022;
  Mesa-Lago 2022 *Cuba's New Economy: Towards a Mixed Economy*; Reuters
  archive (Marc Frank, 1 January 2021).

## Threshold evaluation

- Pre-registered threshold: `>=2 major monetary-regime changes
  (redenomination, FX-regime switch, or >100% annualised inflation
  episode) in a 30-year window`.
- 1993-2023 window: 3 events (1994, 2004, 2021) → MET.

## Robustness

Each event is independently documented in the Gaceta Oficial and
cross-cited in academic monographs (Mesa-Lago, Vidal, Pérez-Lopez). No
event count requires Cuban state CPI data, which is contested for 2021.
