Date: 2026-05-15

## Method

Inspected recent `engine/runs/*` folders with all three artifacts:

- `diagnostics.json`
- `result_card.md`
- `evidence_packet.yaml`

Recent cutoff: diagnostics modified on or after 2026-05-12.

Definitions used here:

- `not_live`: no `positions/*.yaml` entry currently has `linked_hypothesis_id: <run_id>`.
- `map_now`: decisive result, usable hypothesis YAML exists, no obvious duplicate/sign conflict, and the claim can be expressed as a narrow position claim.
- `hold`: do not map now; the result is partial, data-gated, lacks a hypothesis YAML, already live, stale/conflicting, or too fragile for scoreboard movement.
- `improve_panel`: useful target area, but needs a stronger panel or cleaner design before any scoreboard move.

Authoritative verdict source is `diagnostics.json` plus current `result_card.md`. Several evidence packets are older than the latest diagnostics; treat evidence packets as provenance context, not as final verdict authority.
