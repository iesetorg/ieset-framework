# Policy-rate tightening predicts credit-gap compression

**Verdict:** partial - one of the regression or raw-contrast gates clears, but not both.

## Predeclared Test

Policy tightening is a >= 2 pp rise in BIS central-bank policy rates over four quarters; outcome is credit-gap change from t to t+8 quarters.

## Results

- Usable panel: **8,801 observations**, **38 countries**.
- Clustered FE coefficient: **-2.727** (SE 0.796, p=0.0006, 95% CI [-4.288, -1.167]).
- Raw high-minus-normal mean: **-1.487** (-1.644 vs -0.157); treated observations: **982**.

## Caveats

This is a compact local-data panel verdict. It is useful as a falsifiable first tranche, but it should not be read as a structural causal design without stronger identification.
