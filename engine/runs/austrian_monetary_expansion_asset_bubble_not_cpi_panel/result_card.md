# Result card - austrian_monetary_expansion_asset_bubble_not_cpi_panel

**Verdict:** REFUTED - local proxy asset-price coefficient is not positive and significant at p<0.05

## Claim
Broad-money growth should correlate at least 3x more with asset-price growth than with CPI inflation, and the asset coefficient must be positive/significant.

## Exact Local Test
- Usable panel: **147 observations**, **7 countries** (AUS, CAN, CHE, GBR, JPN, SWE, USA).
- Asset-price coefficient: **+0.0445** (SE 0.0434, p=0.305).
- CPI coefficient: **-0.0008** (SE 0.0068, p=0.904).
- Asset/CPI coefficient ratio: **-54.075**.

## Caveat
This is an exact local-data repair, not the ideal specification: WDI broad-money/GDP annual change proxies for broad-money growth, and BIS real residential property prices proxy the asset basket.
