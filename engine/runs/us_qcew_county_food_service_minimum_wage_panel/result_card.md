# US QCEW county food-service employment and minimum wages

**Verdict:** supported - county FE elasticity of food-service employment with respect to state minimum wage is -0.052 (SE 0.015, p=0.000), controlling for total county employment and year shocks.

County and year fixed-effects panel using official BLS QCEW county NAICS 722 employment, BLS county total employment, and USDOL state minimum-wage history.

- Observations: 28892
- Counties: 3049
- States/DC: 53
