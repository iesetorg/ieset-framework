# Household debt-service stress predicts consumption slowdown

**Verdict:** refuted - neither the regression nor raw-contrast gate clears.

## Predeclared Test

High DSR is BIS household DSR >= 12 percent and >= country p75; outcome is the average WDI real private-consumption growth rate in years t+1 and t+2.

## Results

- Usable panel: **3,094 observations**, **17 countries**.
- Clustered FE coefficient: **0.160** (SE 0.358, p=0.6559, 95% CI [-0.543, 0.862]).
- Raw high-minus-normal mean: **-0.666** (0.997 vs 1.663); treated observations: **262**.

## Caveats

This is a compact local-data panel verdict. It is useful as a falsifiable first tranche, but it should not be read as a structural causal design without stronger identification.
